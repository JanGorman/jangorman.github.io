Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All in-app purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. The developer does not receive or store payment information such as credit card details, billing addresses, or Apple ID information.

The app does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or crash reporting tools.

All app data remains on the user’s device unless otherwise required by Apple’s in-app purchase infrastructure.
